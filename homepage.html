About Us

Private Airline Company

We are a Saudi Arabian leading low-cost carrier with a fleet of 34 aircrafts, operating more than 1500 weekly flights to 35 domestic and international destinations. Since our launch, we transported more than 55 million passengers, a number that has steadily grown over the past couple of years. we offer guests best value for money through competitive fares, timely flights and superior customer service which has earned several accolades, including the Middle East’s Leading Low Cost Airline award from the World Travel Awards for six consecutive years (from 2015 till 2020) as well as the prestigious Skytrax award for Middle East’s Best Low Cost Airline in (from 2017 till 2021).
